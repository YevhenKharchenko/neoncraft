About Jewel Scapes

Look, we were just sick of games that felt like work, so we made something that's actually... nice?

You join Sara, a passionate gem hunter, and her adorable companion Puppy Snuffy on a journey through gorgeous worlds with hidden secrets and cool stuff to find. Each level invites you to slow down, match jewels, and enjoy the simple satisfaction of watching the board come alive. There’s no rush. No harsh difficulty spikes. Just puzzles that feel rewarding and comforting at the same time. It’s the kind of game you open “just for a minute” — and end up playing much longer.
